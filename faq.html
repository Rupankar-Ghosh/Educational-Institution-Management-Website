FAQs


1. What is BBA?
BBA is a 3-year undergraduate program focusing on business management, administration, and entrepreneurial skills.

2. What are the eligibility criteria for BBA?
Completion of 10+2 or equivalent with a minimum of 50% marks (varies by institution). Some universities may require English as a mandatory subject.

3. What are the popular specializations in BBA?
Marketing Management Human Resource Management Finance International Business Entrepreneurship Operations Management

4. What is BCA?
BCA is a 3-year undergraduate program that focuses on computer science, programming languages, and IT applications.

5. What are the eligibility criteria for BCA?
Completion of 10+2 with Mathematics or Computer Science as a subject (varies by institution). Minimum marks required range from 45% to 60%.

6. What are the core subjects in BCA?
Programming Languages (C, C++, Java, Python) Database Management Systems (DBMS) Web Development Software Engineering Computer Networks Data Structures

7. What are the career options after BCA?
Software Developer Web Developer System Analyst Database Administrator Network Engineer IT Support Specialist
